Sponsor the Misfits

We need your help to support our young basketball players. The Misfits Basketball Club is a volunteer-run program that helps kids develop their skills and passion for the sport. But we can't do it alone. We rely on fees, fundraisers, and donations to cover our costs.

Your sponsorship will make a big difference for our players. It will help them afford equipment, uniforms, court rentals, insurance, travel, and tournament fees. These expenses add up to more than $500 per player per year, which is hard for many families to pay.

As a thank you for your generosity, we will give you a Team Sponsor Plaque with a team photo that you can display in your office. This will show your customers and community that you care about youth sports. You can also donate gift cards or other items for our raffle baskets.

Please choose a sponsorship level that suits your budget and goals. We appreciate any amount you can give. Thank you for supporting the Misfits Basketball Club!

Policy for Misfits Basketball Sponsorships

The Misfits Basketball Club has adopted the following policy regarding sponsorships:

  1. Any player/parent can freely solicit sponsorships from any person, business, or organization. All money collected will go toward the operating fees for the team.
  2. All team sponsorship money must be used for the gym rental, equipment, uniforms, tournament fees, tournament travel, or player registrations
  3. The Misfits Basketball Club will send a thank you letter to the sponsor
  4. In the event that all the money is not used for the team approved expenses, the remaining money is to be used for cost reduction for Team players or future registration fees.
  5. Misfits Club Basketball banner is going to be a vinyl banner that will be displayed at all team events, scrimmage, league and tournament games. The banner will be viewed by hundreds of people throughout the season.
  6. Our Sponsor Chair will be in contact with sponsors to coordinate logo, business name and graphic requirements for the website and advertising banner.
  7. Please visit the Misfits Basketball Club website for more info.
